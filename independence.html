Somaliland's Claim to Independence

The Case for Independence

Independence is beneficial to Somaliland for a number of reasons.
The citizens of Somaliland cannot be expected to place their trust in a central government which attempted to exterminate them only a short 30 years ago.
Somaliland has proven itself as a rare stable and peaceful democracy in East Africa, rivalling Kenya in the Freedom House democracy rankings.
